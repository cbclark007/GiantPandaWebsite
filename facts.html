Facts About The Giant Panda

Here are a whole bunch of facts about the Giant Panda!

Click on the video on the right for some laughs -->

And here are the facts you're looking for:

  • Has a distinctive black and white coat
  • Mainly lives in bamboo forests high in the mountains of western China
  • Most of their diet consist of bamboo - anywhere from 26 to 84 pounds of it
  • A newborn panda is about the size of a stick of butter, but can grow up to 330 pounds as an adult
  • They are excellent tree climbers
